Our History & Questions

About Us



RealFlex is a family business that started in early 2020 during the pandemic. As many businesses struggled, we wanted to create something stable to secure our family's future — a simple business that requires minimal staff, low maintenance, and can operate in different locations.

Today, RealFlex massage chairs can be found in malls, hotels, and public spaces, providing moments of rest and relaxation for individuals and families.

OUR PRODUCT

What do we sell?

RealFlex massage chairs provide a premium relaxation experience designed to help you unwind and restore your energy. They feature full-body massage functions for the head, neck, back, shoulders, waist, buttocks, and legs, along with a Zero Gravity position for deeper comfort. With Shiatsu, kneading, and tapping modes, RealFlex delivers soothing and effortless relaxation.

Any Questions? We got you.

Have questions about our RealFlex massage chairs? We've got you covered. Browse our frequently asked questions to learn more about our features, usage, and everything you need to know for a relaxing experience.

How much does it cost to use the massage chair? +
The rate is 5 AED per 5 minutes; the maximum session rate is 50 AED for 75 minutes
What are the payment methods accepted? +
Payment can be done directly at the machine after/before clicking the duration of the session, and it can either be cash or card payment
Are the massage chairs safe to use for both Adult and Children? +
Yes, our massage chairs are 100% safe and are regularly maintained to ensure the safety of the customers. However, individuals with serious medical conditions and pregnant women should only use the chair after consulting a doctor for safety. Additionally, if a child is using the chairs, it is advised to have it be supervised by an adult.
What should I do if the massage chair is not working properly? +
If the massage chair is not working properly, kindly contact us immediately using the number displayed on the chair, and our team will assist you as soon as possible.
